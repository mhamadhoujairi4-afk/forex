SMC or Smart Money Concept is a trading method that focuses on understanding the movement of smart money in the market, such as banks and market makers. This idea came from the experience of traders who noticed how large institutions move prices to collect liquidity and control market direction. One of the well-known people who helped develop this concept is Michael Huddleston (ICT), who explained techniques like Order Blocks and Liquidity. The course is divided into several levels, starting from the basics up to advanced strategies, so each person can progress according to their own level. After enrolling, there will be a VIP Telegram channel for members, providing exclusive content and direct support. The course also includes a referral system, where you can earn benefits by bringing people to register or open trading accounts. In the advanced stages, after mastering the strategies, there is an opportunity to apply for funded accounts, allowing you to trade with company capital without risking your own money. In addition to the lessons, we regularly provide market analysis on major assets such as Gold, EUR/USD, GBP/USD, US100 (Nasdaq), and US30 (Dow Jones) to help members stay updated on key trading opportunities.
